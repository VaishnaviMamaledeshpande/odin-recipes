Vada Pav

Main Ingredients

  • For the Batata Vada (Potato Filling + Batter):
    • Potatoes: 3 medium (boiled and mashed)
    • Green chili: 1 (finely chopped)
    • Ginger: 1 inch (grated)
    • Garlic: 4 cloves (crushed)
    • Mustard seeds: ½ tsp
    • Turmeric powder: ¼ tsp
    • Curry leaves: 6 to 8
    • Coriander leaves: 2 tbsp (chopped)
    • Salt: to taste
    • Oil: for tempering and deep frying
  • For the Batter:
    • Besan (gram flour): ¾ cup
    • Turmeric: a pinch
    • Baking soda: a pinch (optional)
    • Salt: to taste
    • Water: as needed for thick batter
  • For Dry Garlic Chutney (Optional):
    • Garlic : 5 cloves
    • Dry red chilies : 2
    • Grated coconut : 2 tbsp (dry or fresh)
    • Salt : to taste
    • Oil : 1 tsp
  • For Serving:
    • Pav buns : 4
    • Green chutney or tamarind chutney : optional
    • Fried green chilies : optional

How to Prepare Tomato Rice

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan.
  2. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped green chili, ginger, garlic, and curry leaves.
  4. Add turmeric and sauté for a few seconds.
  5. Add boiled and mashed potatoes and salt.
  6. Mix well and cook for 2/3 minutes.
  7. Add coriander leaves and let the mixture cool.
  8. Make lemon-sized balls from the mixture.
  9. In a bowl, mix besan, turmeric, salt, and baking soda.
  10. Add water slowly to make a thick, smooth batter.
  11. Heat oil for deep frying.
  12. Dip each potato ball in the besan batter and coat well.
  13. Deep fry until golden and crispy.
  14. Drain on a paper towel.
  15. For dry garlic chutney, roast garlic, red chilies, and coconut.
  16. Blend with salt into a coarse powder (no water).
  17. Slice pav buns horizontally without cutting all the way through.
  18. Apply dry garlic chutney or green chutney inside.
  19. Place a hot vada between the pav.
  20. Serve with fried green chili or extra chutney.
