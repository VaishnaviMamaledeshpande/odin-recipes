Tomato Rice

Main Ingredients

  • Cooked rice: 2 cups (preferably cooled, leftover rice works best)
  • Tomatoes: 2 large or 3 medium (chopped finely or pureed)
  • Onion: 1 medium (sliced or chopped)
  • Green chili: 2
  • Ginger-garlic paste:1 tsp

How to Prepare Tomato Rice

  1. Prepare the rice: If you’re cooking fresh rice, let it cool slightly and fluff it up. (Use 1 cup raw rice = ~2 cups cooked)
  2. Tempering (Tadka): In a pan:
    1. Heat 2 tbsp oil or ghee.
    2. Add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
    3. Add cumin seeds, urad dal (if using), and fry till light golden.
    4. Add curry leaves and green chilies.
  3. Sauté onions & spices
  4. Cook tomatoes
  5. Mix in rice, garnish and serve
